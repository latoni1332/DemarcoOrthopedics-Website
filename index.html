Dr. Charles A. DeMarco, M.D.

Board Certified Orthopedic Surgeon

Dr. Charles A. DeMarco is a board-certified orthopedic surgeon specializing in sports-related injuries and arthroscopic and open surgery of the shoulder, knee, elbow, and ankle. He has over thirty years' experience as an orthopedic surgeon. After earning his medical degree from the State University of New York Health Science Center at Brooklyn, Dr. DeMarco completed his orthopedic residency at SUNY Downstate Medical Center and Kings County Hospital where he recieved his trauma training. He also successfully completed a fellowship in sports medicine at Pennsylvania State University. In addidtion, Dr. DeMarco served as the Director of the Orthopedic Surgery Residency Program at Staten Island University Hospital.

Dr. DeMarco has been qualified as an expert witness in many New York Supreme Court cases and in various administrative hearings. He is highly respected among his peers and is often called upon to consult in complex trauma and sports medicine cases.
